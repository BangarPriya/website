About Us..


Welcome to JewelBells, your number one source for all types of women jewellery, ie: necklaces, bangles, rings... We're dedicated to giving you the very best of women jewellery, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2010 by partners(Komal, Akash, Sukhwinder) , JewelBells has come a long way from its beginnings in a starting location, ie: home office, Kumar Mandi, Ludhiana. When We first started out, our passion for jewellery, ie: creating & providing new collection of women jewellery to women , drove us to jewellery designing field, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Ludhiana. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
